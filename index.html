About Me

Hei, I'm Lars Kristian Dugstad, a passionate software developer and machine learning engineer based in Frankfurt am Main. I am a digitial native and enthusiastic about AI and cloud development. My personal projects - from home automation with microelectronics, 3D printing and robot building to iOS apps and AI cloud solutions - reflect my deep belief that innovation is born at the intersection of curiosity and practical application. This curiosity drives me to continuously search for innovative solutions, be it by learning new programming languages, experimenting with AI in the cloud or developing smart technologies that make everyday life easier. When I'm not building something new, you'll likely find me sailing or spending time with my dog, collecting fresh inspiration for my next ideas.
